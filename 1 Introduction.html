1.0 INTRODUCTION

Washingtonâ€™s War is a two player, strategic level simulation of the American Revolution. By way of the game mechanics, the players attempt to initiate, control, weather or exploit a wide variety of diplomatic, political, cultural and military events in order to either help the United States win its independence or to retain the 13 colonies as British dominions.
